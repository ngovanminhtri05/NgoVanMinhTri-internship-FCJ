**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AWS Foundations and Command Line Mastery](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Architecting VPC & Secure Compute](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Managed Databases & Storage](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [High Availability & Auto Scaling](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Monitoring & Observability](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Content Delivery & Edge Security](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Review knowledge for mid-term test](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Project Kickoff - Smart Office IoT Core](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Smart Office - Rules Engine & Data Persistence](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Smart Office - Serverless API & Visualization](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Final project completion and presentation](1.12-week12/)

**Week 13:** [Coursera AWS Certification](1.13-week13/)
